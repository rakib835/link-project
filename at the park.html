At the Park

Holland Park real estate

With over 16 years’ experience gained from specialist recruitment and real estate roles, Tim Stranks will be joining Holland Park as Head of Real Estate specialising in recruiting Senior to Executive level roles within the Commercial Real Estate market. Tim has successfully led talent attraction projects across multiple business sectors through utilising his ability to listen to requirements, identify the best solution and develop a strong and strategic partnership with all parties throughout the process whilst being transparent and diligent. Often retained to identify key development talent for his clients, Tim’s focus is across UK and Europe, and with an unrivalled network in leading REIT’s, PE Funds, Investors, Developers, Landed Estates and Owner Occupiers, he is uniquely positioned to identify and attract previously untapped talent.

Holland park ex pro

Ed Coulson and James Stokes, who are both professional rugby players, have joined Holland Park as Consultants. Ed and James are a very important first step in Holland Park Ex Pro, a new offering Holland Park provides in business services relating to the legal sector. Holland Park Ex Pro works with professional athletes and service personnel as they look to transition into the corporate world. We believe the discipline, drive, resilience and skills of professional athletes and service personnel will benefit not only the individuals concerned but also bring an array of benefits to law firms and the legal sector generally.
