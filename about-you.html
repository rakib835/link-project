About You

Whether you are a law firm looking for talent or an individual looking for a change in career, we carry out bespoke search and placement. You will receive a personal, dedicated service from our team of Consultants who will listen to what you want and find that for you. You will receive experienced, impartial and professional advice; advice for you on what is best for you.

Clients

IT'S NOT OUR BUSINESS, IT'S YOURS.

You might be a law firm, financial institution or corporate. You might be looking to hire one lawyer, a team or open an office. Whatever your requirements we work closely with you to understand exactly what you are looking for (and why) so that we can identify, discretely approach and secure the best and most suitable candidate(s) or team. We will always listen and work collaboratively with you. Our Consultants have undertaken successful searches for US and City law firms both here and abroad.

Candidates

IT’S NOT OUR CAREER, IT’S YOURS.

You might be a partner, counsel, senior associate, associate or newly qualified. You might be an in-house lawyer. Whoever you are and wherever you are working, we will work with you to understand you, your experience, career plans, goals and objectives so that we can help and support you make the next step in your career. We will never push you into a role that is not right for you and will always give you our honest advice. Our Consultants have placed candidates into US and City law firms both here and abroad.
